>>> SCORING SYSTEM OVERVIEW <<<

SIMPLE FOR JUDGES: All categories use the same 1-5 rating scale

WEIGHTED BEHIND SCENES: Different categories worth different amounts

TWO AWARDS: Technical Excellence + People's Choice

[[[ SCORING CATEGORIES ]]]

1. TASTE & FLAVOR - Weight: ×10 (50 points max)

Judge how the drink tastes and whether flavors work well together

  • 5 = AMAZING - Would order this again, perfect balance
  • 4 = Very Good - Really enjoyable, well-balanced
  • 3 = Good - Solid cocktail, nothing wrong
  • 2 = Not Great - Some issues but drinkable
  • 1 = Poor - Hard to finish

Example: Judge gives 4 → 4 × 10 = 40 points

2. PRESENTATION - Weight: ×4 (20 points max)

Judge the visual appeal, garnishes, and overall styling

  • 5 = WOW FACTOR - Take a picture even though it will likely just languish on your phone for eternity
  • 4 = Impressive - Multiple elements, thoughtful styling
  • 3 = Nice - Well-presented, attractive
  • 2 = Some Effort - One garnish or nice glass
  • 1 = Basic - Just a drink in a glass

Example: Judge gives 3 → 3 × 4 = 12 points

3. WORKMANSHIP - Weight: ×3 (15 points max)

Judge the care, effort, and skill put into making the drink

  • 5 = PERFECT - Meticulous preparation, silky smooth texture, no chunks or unblended bits
  • 4 = Skilled - Clear effort and skill, smooth mouthfeel, proper technique execution
  • 3 = Good - Well-made with care, pleasant texture, ingredients properly integrated
  • 2 = Needs Work - Some carelessness, chunky bits, rushed preparation
  • 1 = Poor - Sloppy mixing, lumpy/gritty, minimal effort evident

Example: Judge gives 4 → 4 × 3 = 12 points

4. CREATIVITY - Weight: ×3 (15 points max)

Judge the originality and uniqueness of the concept or ingredients

  • 5 = GENIUS - Mind-blowing innovation, never seen this before
  • 4 = Very Unique - Unexpected ingredients or creative concept
  • 3 = Creative - Interesting combination or original idea
  • 2 = Slight Twist - Minor variation on common cocktail
  • 1 = Basic - Standard mix everyone knows (rum & coke level)

Example: Judge gives 5 → 5 × 3 = 15 points

TECHNICAL TOTAL: 100 POINTS MAXIMUM

Example calculation: 32 + 15 + 20 + 10 = 77 points

>>> PEOPLE'S CHOICE AWARD <<<

PEOPLE'S CHOICE

Each judge picks ONE cocktail as their "favorite overall"

Most votes wins - captures the "I'd order this again" factor

Key insight: Sometimes the most technically perfect drink isn't your favorite!

Note: These votes also add +3 bonus points to final scoring

>>> BEST COCKTAIL WINNER <<<

BEST COCKTAIL

Based on 100-point weighted scoring system PLUS people's choice bonus

Technical Score (up to 100) + People's Choice Bonus (+3 per vote)

Rewards: skill, technique, balance, presentation quality AND popularity

[[[ HOW SCORING WORKS ]]]

Technical Scoring: Weighted 100-point system based on taste, presentation, execution, and creativity

Favorite Vote Bonus: +3 points for each "favorite overall" vote received from judges

Final Score: Technical Score + (Number of Favorite Votes × 3)

Example:

Cocktail A: 85 technical points + 3 favorite votes = 85 + 9 = 94 total

Cocktail B: 92 technical points + 1 favorite vote = 92 + 3 = 95 total

Winner: Cocktail B (higher final score)
